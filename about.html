About Us

Introduction

Wowzers CO. is an Audio Visual company providing services to the events and marketing industry. The company was established in 2015 and has proven itself as a trusted service provider.

We are a dedicated team of event managers, photographers, videographers, visual technicians and graphic designers led by the Director, Owner and Founder, Nur Hasyim Muhammad.

We have strong, healthy relationships with marketing companies, event planners, hotels and conference centers which serve both the private and public sectors. We pride ourselves in having a fresh, innovative approach, resulting in delivering a world class experience with every project we execute.

Our team guarantees an exclusive and individual service. We offer personal consultations with highly qualified creative directors, photo-editors, image and video producers, story-boarders, graphic Illustrators, stylists, make-up artists, hair stylists and photographers.

Wowzers works independently or synergistically, depending on the clients requirements. We use our experience and our know-how in a efficient way for the production of visual media and in the Brand management.

The Owner

Nur Hasyim Muhammad

Nur Hasyim Muhammad is the Owner cum Director at Wowzers Co. Prior to Wowzers, Hasyim was an Assistant Director at BigHit Labels Korea after finishing his studies in Seoul.

He has contributed to a number of different industry publication including Bloomberg and Forbes.

He has a Bachelor in IT (Multimedia) from University of Malaya and a Master's Degree in Multimedia from Seoul National University.

In his spare time, he cooks and is a self-proclaimed big-eater.
